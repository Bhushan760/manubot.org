At a glance
Manubot is a workflow and set of tools for the future of scholarly publishing. Write your manuscript in markdown, track it with your favorite version control, automatically convert it to .html, .pdf, or .docx, and deploy it to your destination of choice.

The Best Parts
Open, collaborative, versioned
Author your manuscript the open-source way. Let anyone propose changes. Iteratively review, discuss, and refine with collaborators. Keep track of versions, to-dos, revisions, and who wrote what and when. All in a central location.
Automated and continuous
Never use a reference manager again. Have your citations generated and numbered for you. Provide a DOI, ISBN, PubMed ID, url, etc., and let Manubot do the rest. See your manuscript update right away as you make changes. Test and deploy automatically with continuous integration.
Beautiful and interactive
Static is out, dynamic is in. Publish your paper with the contemporary look and interactive features people have come to love and expect from modern webpages: tooltips, public annotations, table of contents, figure viewer, and more.
Free and open-source
There's no need to pay to produce brilliant manuscripts. Manubot is 100% free and open-source. Its tools are developed and supported by a community of responsive and passionate software developers and researchers.
